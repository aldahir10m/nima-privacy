Privacy Policy for Nima

Effective date: June 30, 2026

Nima ("Nima", "we", "us", or "our"), operated by Aldahir Mora Alvarez, provides a personal expense and budgeting application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how we use it, who we share it with, and the choices and rights you have.

We built Nima to be private by default. We do not sell your data, we do not use it for advertising, and we do not track you across other apps or websites.

Summary

  • We collect the information needed to run your account and sync your data across your devices: your email address, the financial entries you create, a user identifier, your subscription status, and basic analytics and crash diagnostics.
  • Your data is isolated to your account and is not accessible to other users.
  • Voice recordings and receipt photos are never stored by Nima. They are processed to extract a draft transaction and then discarded.
  • You can delete your account and data from within the App at any time.

Information we collect

We collect the following categories of data. Each is linked to your account and is used to provide and improve the App. None of it is used to track you.

Data What it is Why we collect it
Email address The address you use to create and sign in to your account (directly or via Apple/Google sign-in). To create your account, authenticate you, and send essential account or security messages.
Financial information The transactions, amounts, categories, budgets, and savings goals you enter. This is the core function of the App. We store it so you can view and sync your records across devices.
User identifier A unique account identifier we generate for you. To associate your data with your account and to operate the App.
Product interaction (usage) data Anonymous-where-possible events about how features are used (for example, that a transaction was created), with no transaction content. To understand which features are used and improve the App.
Diagnostics Crash reports and performance data when the App encounters an error. To detect, diagnose, and fix crashes and performance problems.
Purchase history Your subscription and trial status. To provide and manage your subscription and access.
Coarse / precise location Requested only during onboarding to determine your country. We use it once to set your default currency and language. We do not store your coordinates; only the resulting currency and country preference is saved. You can deny this prompt and set your currency manually.

Information we do not collect

  • Voice recordings and receipt photos are not stored. When you use voice or photo entry, the audio or image is processed solely to produce a draft transaction for you to confirm, and is then discarded. It is never written to our servers, logs, or database. See "Voice and image input" below.
  • Your location coordinates are not stored. Only the derived currency/country preference is saved.
  • We do not collect data for advertising or cross-app tracking, and the App contains no advertising or third-party advertising SDKs.

How we use your information

We use the information above to:

  • Provide the App's core functionality (recording, categorizing, and syncing your finances).
  • Authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • Operate and manage your subscription and free trial.
  • Diagnose crashes and improve performance and usability.
  • Comply with our legal obligations.

Where required by law, our legal bases are the performance of our contract with you (providing the App), your consent (for example, the optional location prompt), and our legitimate interest in maintaining and improving the App.

Voice and image input

When you add a transaction by voice or photo:

  • On-device transcription and text recognition are used first where available, so your audio or image may never leave your device.
  • If a cloud service is needed (for example, on-device transcription is unavailable on your device), the single recording or image is sent to our processor solely to produce a draft transaction.

For any cloud processing, our processors are contractually and by configuration bound so that your data is not used to train their models, is retained no longer than necessary to provide the service and any short abuse-monitoring window (except where a provider is required to retain it to comply with law or legal process), and is never used for cross-app tracking or advertising. The audio or image itself is never stored by Nima; only the structured draft you confirm is saved.

Data storage, security, and retention

Your data is stored on secure cloud infrastructure located in the United States and is isolated to your account through row-level security, so it is not accessible to other users. We retain your data for as long as your account is active.

The App is local-first: a copy of your data is also stored on your device so the App works offline.

Deleting your account and data

You can delete your account at any time from Settings → Delete account inside the App. When you do:

  • Your data is removed from the App immediately.
  • Your data is retained for 30 days and then permanently deleted from our servers. This grace period exists so an accidental deletion can be recovered; after 30 days the deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Your rights

Depending on where you live (for example, under the GDPR or CCPA/CPRA), you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. You can exercise deletion directly in the App as described above, and you can contact us at aldahir10@gmail.com for any other request. We do not sell your personal information.

Children's privacy

Nima is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal data from them. If you believe a child has provided us personal data, contact us and we will delete it.

International data transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than the one in which you live, including by the service providers listed above. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards for such transfers.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the effective date above and, for material changes, provide notice within the App or by other appropriate means.
